# Module 1 - Concept Checkpoints

Answer these without notes.

---

## Section A - Core Understanding

1. Explain linear regression in one sentence without using formulas.
2. What does the parameter `w` control? What does `b` control?
3. Why can two models with similar training loss have different validation performance?
4. What is the difference between error, loss, and cost?
5. When would you prefer MAE over MSE?

---

## Section B - Optimization Reasoning

6. If training loss oscillates wildly, what is your first hypothesis?
7. Why can unscaled features hurt gradient descent?
8. What does it mean if both train and validation losses stay high?
9. What does it mean if train loss drops but validation loss rises?

---

## Section C - Practical Thinking

10. You built a salary predictor and got low MSE, but users complain prediction errors are huge for senior roles. Give two possible reasons.
11. A new feature improves validation from 0.80 to 0.97 instantly. What should you suspect first?
12. Why is a simple linear baseline valuable even when you plan to train deep models?
